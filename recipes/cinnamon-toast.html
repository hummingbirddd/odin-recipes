Cinnamon Sugar Toast

Image by Dale Cruse via flickr, licensed under 2.0 Generic

Description

It's cinnamon, it's toast, it's cinnamon sugar toast!

If you need a recipe for how to make this... well here's one, but really?

Ratio of cinnamon sugar is going to vary by taste, as will amount of mixture sprinkled on toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 Tbsp salted butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Toast the bread. You now have toast.
  2. Mix the cinnamon and sugar.
  3. Butter the toast.
  4. Sprinkle cinnamon-sugar mixture onto toast generously.
  5. Monch.

Tip: If you only have 1/3-1/4 jar of cinnamon left, you can pour sugar into the jar, leaving just enough room to shake and stir. Use for future cinnatoasts.
